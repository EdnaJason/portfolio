The Best Movies According to Edna

My top 3 movies from recent watch

The Wild Robot

A heartwarming movie. It made me go through so many emotions. It showed the depth of a mother's love.

Kishkindha Kaandam

This movie had an amazing way of story telling. The shots and the music were really good. The actors did a fantastic job too.

Aavesham

The most fun I've had watching a movie in a while. The movie showcased some of the best acting by Fahadh Faasil.
